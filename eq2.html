 Intro: Systems and Networks

What is a system?

  • A collection of separate things brought together to serve a particular purpose, usually sharing resources.
  • School systems, D league systems, Sound systems, etc.

What is a network?

  • A group of individual devices that are connected and able to share information
  • Different from a system in that networks usually just communicate instead of having a common goal and shared resources like a system.
